SOURCE
------
RadioReference.com — Buncombe County, NC (database last updated 2026-06-18):
  References/Buncombe County, North Carolina (NC) Scanner Frequencies and Radio
  Frequency Reference.pdf

The CHIRP list is a DELIBERATE SUBSET focused on the owner's Fairview / SE-Buncombe
area + countywide fire/EMS + the standard state/SAR channels. The full RR database
lists many more agencies (sheriff, every VFD, municipalities, schools, the P25
trunked systems) that are intentionally NOT carried here.

All channels are RECEIVE-ONLY monitoring (Duplex=off) and programmed CARRIER SQUELCH
(no tone) so all traffic is heard — standard scanner practice. The verified PL/DPL
tones are recorded below for reference only (needed solely if transmitting, which the
owner does not do on these). Mode NFM (RR: "FMN") throughout.

NOTES
-----
- All 21 frequencies + descriptions match the RR database exactly (2026-06-29 check).
- Slot 173 (Skyland Rebroadcast) re-uses 155.565 (= Fireground 1) on a separate
  transmitter and tone (167.9 PL) — intentional duplicate.
- Primary Buncombe dispatch is now P25 trunked; these conventional channels are the
  paging / fireground / VFD / backup / mutual-aid freqs that remain analog-monitorable.
- Two channels use DPL (DCS) rather than PL: 159.3525 (D125) and 152.405 (D411).
- VFD subset = Fairview/Reynolds/Riceville/Skyland (the owner's SE-Buncombe area).
  Other Buncombe VFDs in RR (Barnardsville, Broad River, Enka, French Broad,
  Leicester, Reems Creek, Swannanoa, Upper Hominy, West Buncombe, Woodfin) were
  intentionally omitted.
