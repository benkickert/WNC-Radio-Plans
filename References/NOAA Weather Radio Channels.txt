==============================================================================
NOAA WEATHER RADIO (NWR) CHANNELS
==============================================================================
Source: NOAA / National Weather Service - NOAA Weather Radio All Hazards
        (NWR) national frequency list. Local station confirmed via
        radiostation.info / Broadcastify. Captured 2026-07-01.
Purpose: definitive source-of-record for `CHIRP Lists/NOAA weather.csv`
         (slots 91-97).

NWR broadcasts continuous weather + all-hazards alerts on 7 VHF frequencies
nationwide. These are RECEIVE-ONLY: program Duplex = "off", no tone. Any radio
that covers 162 MHz can listen; only the station serving your area transmits.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The 7 NWR frequencies
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Frequency (MHz)   Baseline name
162.400           WX1AVL   <- serves the Asheville / WNC area (see below)
162.425           WX2
162.450           WX3
162.475           WX4
162.500           WX5
162.525           WX6
162.550           WX7

(Note: these are named sequentially by frequency in this repo. The "WX1-7"
scanner-preset numbering used by some receivers orders them differently; the
frequencies are what matter, so the repo keeps a simple ascending mapping.)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Local station serving the owner's area (Fairview / Asheville, WNC)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Callsign:   WXL56
Frequency:  162.400 MHz
Transmitter: Mt. Pisgah (covers Asheville and surrounding WNC)
This is why the 162.400 row is named "WX1AVL" - it is the local Asheville NWR
station. The other six frequencies are carried so the radio can pick up
whichever NWR transmitter is in range while traveling.

Source for the local station:
  - radiostation.info/weather/north-carolina/WXL56/  (WXL56, 162.400, Asheville)
  - Broadcastify "Asheville Area NOAA Weather Radio WXL56" live feed
