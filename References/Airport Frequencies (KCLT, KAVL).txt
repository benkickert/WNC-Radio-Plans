AIRPORT ATC FREQUENCY REFERENCE — KCLT (Charlotte) & KAVL (Asheville)
=====================================================================
Verified: 2026-06-29
Purpose:  Source-of-record for the airband monitoring CHIRP Lists
          (CHIRP Lists/CLT Airport.csv, CHIRP Lists/AVL Airport.csv).
          Everything in those lists must trace back to an entry here.

All frequencies are AM-mode, receive-only (airband: 118.000-136.975 MHz VHF;
225-400 MHz UHF military). Listening only — these are not legal/possible to
transmit on with the fleet. UHF entries (225-400) are receivable only on some
radios (see ai-notes/radios.md).

SOURCES
-------
- FAA Chart Supplement, via AirNav:
    https://www.airnav.com/airport/KCLT
    https://www.airnav.com/airport/KAVL
- CLT approach/departure sector labels (TRACON sectors + arrival gates):
    https://cltflyer.com/airport-frequencies/
    (derived from the Charlotte ATCT 7110.65 SOP, ZTL ARTCC)
- KAVL approach sector heading split: FAA Chart Supplement / ATC SOP
    (124.65 covers hdg 160-339; 125.80 covers hdg 340-159).

NOTES ON SECTOR LABELS
----------------------
- CLT: CHIRP Lists/CLT Airport.csv was reconciled to this reference on
  2026-06-29 — sector labels corrected (126.150 = Final; 125.350 = South
  gate/BTSEY; 135.600 = North gate/JOOLS; 128.325 = East Low; 134.750 =
  North Low) and the original unverified arrival-fix lists removed.
- KAVL: the approach split is by aircraft heading, not a clean
  "East/West." 124.650 = hdg 160-339, 125.800 = hdg 340-159. The
  UHF<->VHF sector pairing (269.575 / 351.800) is not published in
  these sources; treat the E/W labels as approximate.
