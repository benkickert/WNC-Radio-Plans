Provenance note (2026-07-13)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The three repeaters above are carried in `CHIRP Lists/KY route gap-fillers
(RepeaterBook).csv`, which is what `Complete Radio Plans/AVL to KY.csv` pulls from.
(Previously `NewprtTN` was pulled from this document straight into the plan, which
skipped the CHIRP-List tier of the provenance chain; the list closes that gap.)

Tone-mode standard applied to all three: RepeaterBook lists an **uplink (access)
tone but no downlink tone**, which cannot justify TSQL -> use `Tone` (encode-only),
carrier RX, so the repeater is never muted. Same rule as the WNC ham audit.
